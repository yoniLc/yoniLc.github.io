Yoni Choukroun

I am a Computer Science Ph.D. candidate at Tel Aviv University, working in the Deep Learning Lab under the supervision of Prof. Lior Wolf. I obtained my BSc in Computer Engineering from the Technion and my M.Sc. in Computer Science from the Technion under the supervision of Prof. Ron Kimmel. I am also a team lead AI research scientist at Huawei Israel Research Center.

My research interests are centered around machine/deep learning, computer vision, information theory, and numerical optimization.
